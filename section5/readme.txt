Codes to estimate models in section 5 of "Dealing With Misspecification in Structural Macroeconometric Models" by Fabio Canova and Christian Matthes

The two subfolders in this folder contain the codes for the two applications in section 5 of the paper:

1. Measuring the marginal propensity to consume

- run main_QE_consumption.m in consumption_example



2. The effects of technology shocks

- run main_QE_technology.m in technology_example
